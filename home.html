Where does your passion lie?


Before you answer that question, keep in mind that a passion is something that brings you pleasure, something you genuinely love doing. It's a natural interest which you easily excel in.

  • Music
  • Nature
  • Movement
  • Interpersonal
  • Intrapersonal
  • Logic
  • Linguistic
  • Visual
  • Teaching

Music

  • Enjoy listening to music
  • Learn better by listening
  • Sharp sense to what enters the ear
  • Memorize pieces of music quickly

Nature

  • Enjoy time in nature
  • Love plants and animals
  • Understand how elements react in nature
  • Understand how most animals react
  • Have survival skills

Movement

  • Enjoy moving all around
  • Powerful ability to move individual muscles
  • Repeat movements
  • Love body practice

Interpersonal

  • Understand relationships between people
  • Quickly read body language
  • Good at holding conversations
  • Understand human nature and interactions

Intrapersonal

  • Deep understanding of oneself
  • Spot what needs work concerning oneself
  • Reflect on and adjust oneself
  • Self-improvement

Logic

  • Reason well
  • Interest in maths and science
  • Interest in the truth
  • Act on reason more than emotion
  • Search for correct answers not right ones
  • Excellent at solving technical problems

Linguistic

  • Deep understanding of languages and the emotions that comes with certain words
  • Articulate
  • Vast vocabulary
  • Enjoy reading and listening to great speakers
  • Craft engaging messages
  • More influentional than average

Visual

  • Spot what looks pleasant to eye
  • Imagine complex images in their heads
  • Recreate images on paper

Teaching

  • Turn complicated subjects to simple understandable words
  • Spot what's right or wrong in other's field
  • Sense of satisfaction seeing their students grow
Figure out which one of these abilities interests you the most, and then try to think about what career path makes use of those abilities and allows you to make a good living while doing something you’re actually interested in which will make your work life more fun & enjoyable .
